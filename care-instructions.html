Take care of your garment

Due to its natural material, it just needs a little bit of love and some extra care, because loved clothed last.

Hand wash cold or machine wash cold using delicate cycle + air dry flat. Do not bleach & do not tumble dry.

Low heat and gently press iron - too much direct heat may scorch the fabric. Recommended to iron inside out.

Please note that tencel could potentially shrink about 2-3% with the first washing and will resist shrinking from then on. All GG garments have already been through one post print gentle wash cycle.

We encourage you to wear your clothes more and to wash less so as to reduce your impact on the environment.

We hope you appreciate our commitment to being as eco-responsible as we can with our pieces the care that has gone into each garment.
