Our Karang Kimono, is inspired by the treasure that Mama Ocean holds: Karang, which means corals in Bahasa Indonesia, and by its beautiful natural pink beaches that this truly mesmerizing country hosts, evoking visions of tropical island lifestyle.

Embodying femininity and Nature’s beauty and effortlessly flowing with the breeze, this elegant with a boho chic touch kimono is the perfect cover up for your chilling days by the pool or at the beach for your summer holidays. Or why not dressing it up wearing it tight up with jeans or white trousers The karang Kimono is not only versatile and a key piece to have in your day to day, holiday and travel wardrobe but also feels so soft and comfortable to wear. This kimono sublimates your outfit with its watercolour illustration.

Care Instructions +

Due to its natural fabric, it just needs a little bit of love and some extra care and to keep its shape and luster.

Hand wash cold or machine wash cold using delicates cycle + air dry flat.

Do not bleach & do not tumble dry.

Low heat and gently press iron - too much direct heat may scorch the fabric.

Recommended to iron inside out

Please note tencel could potencially shrink about 2-3% with the first washing and will resist shrinking from then on. All GG garments have already been through one gentle wash cycle.

Looking after your clothes properly will make them last longer
