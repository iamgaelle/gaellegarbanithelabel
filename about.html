Sustainability

Gaelle Garbani brand is a eco-conscious brand, or as I like to call it, a clothing brand with a conscious.
I couldn’t in good conscience to create and produce a brand in fashion whilst turning a blind eye to what the social and environmental impacts the fashion industry is responsible for. Therefore, my passion for Nature and the desire to preserve it at best fuelled the creation of the Gaelle Garbani brand, hoping to make a little difference and act as a little manifestation for the future of fashion.

But sustainability is a journey, and as a small fashion starter, we are doing our best to do our part with our current means. As much as I would like to claim Gaelle Garbani to be a sustainable brand, no brand can genuinely and truly be “100% sustainable” but it as business owner, it is our duty to act consciously and pick the battles that we can win with the means we have in our current power, until you grow stronger and more knowledgeable and can win more battles.
